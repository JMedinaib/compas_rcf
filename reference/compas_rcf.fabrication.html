compas_rcf.fabrication package

compas_rcf.fabrication

Runners & fabrication objects

This modules contains runners; scripts for running a fabrication process using a digital fabrication method. It also contains fabrication objects, most significantly compas_rcf.fabrication.ClayBullet which is our data representation of our discrete building blocks.
